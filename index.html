Our Story

Where passion meets flavour and tradition.

Established in 2010 as a small home business, Butterfly Chocolate has blossomed into one of Kuwait’s favourite destinations for luxury desserts. From the outset, we’ve been committed to developing premium chocolates and pastries that delight the senses. Over the years our dedication to quality, innovation and elegance has earned us an impeccable reputation.

As our family of customers grew, so did our ambitions. In 2018 we undertook a complete transformation — upgrading our facilities and bringing on expert chefs and experienced management. By October 2020, after navigating the challenges of the pandemic, we proudly revealed a refreshed logo and a brand‑new menu, reflecting our drive to continuously evolve.

At Butterfly Chocolate, we believe in keeping our promises: quality, taste and elegance. Join us on a journey of indulgence.
